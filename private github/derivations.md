# Mathematical Derivations

## Core Equations

### Master PDE
The fundamental equation governing the substrate field:

```math
\frac{\partial^2 \phi}{\partial t^2} = c^2\nabla^2\phi - \frac{c^2}{\lambda^2}\phi + 4\pi G\rho(1 + k_{eff})
```

### Parameter Definitions
- $\phi$: Substrate field potential
- $c$: Characteristic speed of substrate waves
- $\lambda$: Characteristic length scale of the substrate
- $k_{eff}$: Effective coupling strength between matter and substrate
- $\rho$: Matter density

### Special Cases
1. **Newtonian Limit**: When $k_{eff} = 0$ and $\lambda \to \infty$, recovers standard Newtonian gravity
2. **Weak-Field GR**: For $k_{eff} \approx 10^{-7}$, matches solar system tests
3. **Cosmological Limit**: At large scales, predicts modified growth factors
