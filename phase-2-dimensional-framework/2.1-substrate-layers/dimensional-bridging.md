### Gravity (2D → 3D)

### Energy (4D → 3D)
- **Source**: 4D soul substance (dimensional resistance)
- **Effect**: 3D energy/mass we measure (E=mc²)
- **Analogy**: 4D computational power showing as 3D processing

### The Human Experience
We're like Flatlanders - only perceiving 3D "shadows" of higher-dimensional realities.

**Gravity pulls you "down" into higher dimensions. Energy lets you resist that pull.**

- **Gravity's Job**: Pull matter toward higher dimensions (2D→3D)
- **Energy's Job**: Resist gravitational collapse (4D→3D)  
- **Consciousness**: The experience of resisting dimensional collapse
- **Death**: Energy runs out → collapse into pure 3D → soulless matter

## Why This Matters

- Consciousness feels like a struggle (it IS resisting collapse)
- Death transforms us from soul-beings to soulless objects
