# Experimental Guidance for Fifth Force Search

## Target Parameters
- Mediator mass: m_S = 1.973e-04 eV
- Coupling: α_S = 6.324e-35
- Force range: 1.0 mm

## Required Apparatus
1. Force sensitivity: 5e-16 N (5× better than current)
2. Distance control: 0.1-10 mm range, ±1 μm precision
3. Test masses: Various materials for baryon dependence test
4. Environment: Ultra-high vacuum, temperature stability

## Key Signatures
1. Yukawa suppression: Force drops faster than 1/r²
2. Material dependence: Force ∝ (baryon number)²
3. Distance scaling: Characteristic 1/m_S length scale

## Falsification Criteria
Theory ruled out if:
1. No deviation >3σ at 1 mm with 5e-16 N sensitivity
2. Force shows no material dependence
3. Force follows pure 1/r² at all distances
