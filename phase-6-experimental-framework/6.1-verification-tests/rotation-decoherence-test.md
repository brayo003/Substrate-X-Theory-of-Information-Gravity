# Rotation-Induced Decoherence Test

## Core Prediction
\`\`\`
Γ = Γ₀ + kω²
k = 4.5 × 10⁻⁷ s
\`\`\`

## Experimental Predictions
- At ω = 100 rad/s: ΔΓ = 4.5 × 10⁻³ s⁻¹
- At ω = 200 rad/s: ΔΓ = 1.8 × 10⁻² s⁻¹

## Experimental Protocol
1. **System**: Trapped ions (Yb⁺, Ca⁺) or superconducting qubits
2. **Rotation**: Precision platform (0-200 rad/s)
3. **Measurement**: Quantum state tomography
4. **Procedure**: Measure T₂(ω) at multiple rotation rates
5. **Analysis**: Fit to quadratic dependence

## Why This is Revolutionary
- **No other theory** predicts Γ ∝ ω²
- **Standard QM**: No rotation dependence
- **Direct substrate detection** through information current coupling
- **Testable now** with existing quantum technology
