\section{Substrate Master Equation}

\[
\frac{\partial s}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot (s \mathbf{v}_{\text{sub}}) = \alpha E - \beta \nabla \cdot (E \mathbf{v}_{\text{sub}}) + \gamma F - \sigma_{\text{irr}}
\]

Where:
- $s$: Information density in substrate
- $\mathbf{v}_{\text{sub}}$: Substrate flow velocity  
- $E$: Energy density
- $\sigma_{\text{irr}}$: Irreversible information production
